Families and roles

Bud

Seed-state helpers. Simple, foundational, readable, good for first growth and first work.

Shell

Durable and defensive. Best when the task needs stability, packing, persistence, or resistance.

Spark

Fast, energetic, iterative. Best when throughput and momentum matter.

Bloom

Expressive and aesthetic. Best when visual taste, presence, and character are central.

Prism

Analytical and refractive. Best when the task depends on technical reading and precision.

Crest

Authoritative and vertical. Best when the work needs judgment, order, and priority.

Weaver

Linked and orchestrated. Best when the work spans multiple parts and must be connected well.

Shade

Subtle and risk-aware. Best when ambiguity, edge cases, or hidden state matter.

Builder

Use for scaffolding, structure, feature assembly, and structural implementation.

Researcher

Use for exploration, comparison, discovery, and mapping unknowns before action.

Reviewer

Use for critique, validation, standards, and consistency checks.

Operator

Use for execution, dispatch, repeated flows, and throughput work.
